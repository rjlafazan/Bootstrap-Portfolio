About Me

"The only real voyage of discovery consists not in seeking new landscapes but in having new eyes." - Marcel Proust

I am a driven software developer with a passion for technology, fitness, and the great outdoors. My ever expanding skills are built through leadership, education, and personal interests. I take ownership of tasks and work with a strong sense of urgency in all things that require my attention. In my spare time, I enjoy martial arts, backpacking and adventures, hanging out with my 2 year old German shepherd, husky, and akita mix named Ghost, and reading.
